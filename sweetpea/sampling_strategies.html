Sampling Strategies¶

class sweetpea.sampling_stratgies.unigen.UnigenSamplingStrategy¶

Generates trials with some guarantee of uniformity. Unfortunately, this stategy is unlikely to succeed for non-trial designs.

class sweetpea.sampling_stratgies.non_uniform.NonUniformSamplingStrategy¶

Generates trials by repeatedly finding solutions to an experiment design’s constraints, but with no guarantee of uniform coverage.

class sweetpea.sampling_stratgies.uniform_combinatoric.UniformCombinatoricSamplingStrategy¶

Generates trials with a guarantee of uniformity, but currently works only for experiment designs without constraints or derived factors.
